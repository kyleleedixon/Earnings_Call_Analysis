About

Photon is an artificial intelligence tool that analyzes language used in quarterly earnings calls to predict securities class action lawsuit filings before they happen.

By analyzing transcripts and monitoring stock prices following earnings calls, we are able to predict the liklihood of facing a class action securities lawsuit for publicly traded companies.

Quantify lawsuit probability

Stay ahead of the curve by understanding the liklihood of a case filing against your clients.

Visualize stock prices

Analyze stock price movement immediately following an earnings call of interest.

Transcript highlighting

Understand the key words idenfied in an earnings call with via highlighting.

Web based

The Photon software can be accessed from any desktop with an internet connection.
